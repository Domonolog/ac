<h1>UXIS company Accessibility Statement</h1>

Updated: December 2019.

<h2>General</h2>

UXIS company strives to ensure that its services are accessible to people with disabilities. UXIS company has invested a significant amount of resources to help ensure that its website is made easier to use and more accessible for people with disabilities, with the strong belief that website accessibility efforts assist all users and that every person has the right to live with dignity, equality, comfort and independence.

<h2>Accessibility on uxis.company</h2>

uxis.company makes available the <a href="https://UserWay.org" alt="Free Website Accessibility Widget" title="Free Website Accessibility Widget">UserWay's Web Accessibility Widget</a> is powered by a dedicated accessibility server. The software allows uxis.company to improve its compliance with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG 2.1).

<h2>Enabling the Accessibility Menu</h2>

The uxis.company accessibility menu can be enabled by clicking the accessibility menu icon that appears on the corner of the page. After triggering the accessibility menu, please wait a moment for the accessibility menu to load in its entirety.

<h2>Disclaimer</h2>

UXIS company continues its efforts to constantly improve the accessibility of its site and services in the belief that it is our collective moral obligation to allow seamless, accessible and unhindered use also for those of us with disabilities.

<br /><br />

In an ongoing effort to continually improve and remediate accessibility issues, we also regularly scan uxis.company with UserWay's <a href="https://UserWay.org/scanner" title="Free Website Accessibility Scanner">Accessibility Scanner</a> to identify and fix every possible accessibility barrier on our site. Despite our efforts to make all pages and content on uxis.company fully accessible, some content may not have yet been fully adapted to the strictest accessibility standards. This may be a result of not having found or identified the most appropriate technological solution.

<h2>Here For You</h2>

If you are experiencing difficulty with any content on uxis.company or require assistance with any part of our site, please contact us during normal business hours as detailed below and we will be happy to assist.

Why does my business need an Accessibility Statement?

Accessibility statements exist to show site visitors the current status of your website’s accessibility as well as any accessibility-related efforts your business is working on right now.

All new websites are required to meet accessibility standards and publish an accessibility statement, namely websites offering information or services to the public sector.

Accessibility statements help your users to understand the extent to which your site, app, products, and services are accessible. Where barriers or violations exist, the statement must inform users of alternative routes to access.

This information is especially useful to site visitors with disabilities who may need specific accommodations. Your statement should also share where visitors can get more information or leave feedback if they encounter any accessibility barriers or limitations, including how to contact the website owner if they encounter any accessibility violations.

Newly published websites must have already published an accessibility statement by September 2019, while older websites have had until January 1, 2020 to comply.
